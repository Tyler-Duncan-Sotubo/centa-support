## Essential attendance features

### Real-time attendance & insights

Track check-ins and check-outs across all locations and teams.

- Live status: **on-time, late, overtime, and absent**
- Unified dashboard for **biometric, web, and mobile punches**
- Complete **audit logs** for reviews and compliance

---

### Shifts, scheduling & leave sync

Attendance works seamlessly with shifts and leave to keep records accurate.

- **Smart shift templates** (fixed or rotating)
- Configurable **grace periods** and automatic overtime rules
- Leave and holiday calendars sync directly with attendance

---

### Payroll & compliance ready

Export clean, policy-ready timesheets and reduce disputes.

- **One-click timesheet export** (CSV / XLSX / API)
- **Multi-level approvals** with role-based access
- **Immutable audit trails** for compliance and reviews
